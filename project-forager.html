Forager

Building the future of cross-border freight forwarding.

Role Product Designer
Timeline 2021 - 2022
Services Strategy,Research,Design
Platform Web Application
[Main project hero image]

The Problem

Forager's load board lacked a direct way for carriers to negotiate rates, forcing both sides into inefficient manual processes.

  • Carriers couldn't submit counter-offers above target rates
  • Manual negotiation outside platform delayed bookings
  • No real time visibility into bid status or responses
  • Lack of transparency slowed load coverage decisions

The Solution

I designed a self-service bidding platform that enabled direct negotiation and real time decision making

  • Carrier interface for browsing loads and submitting bids
  • Real time Slack notifications for new bid submissions
  • Forager dashboard to review, counter, and accept bids
  • Automated booking upon bid acceptance

The Impact

The self-service bidding feature transformed how carriers and Foragers negotiated rates, creating a faster and more transparent marketplace.

The new bidding system eliminated the need for manual, offline negotiations that previously delayed load coverage. Carriers could now submit counter-offers directly through the load board, while real-time Slack notifications ensured Foragers could review and respond to bids immediately.

By centralizing the negotiation process within the platform, both carriers and Foragers gained transparency and efficiency. What was once a fragmented, time-consuming back-and-forth became a streamlined workflow that increased carrier engagement and accelerated booking times. The reusable bidding component also laid the foundation for future marketplace enhancements.
