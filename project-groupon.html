UX Design • E-commerce

Groupon

Reimagining the merchant experience to empower local businesses.

Role Product Designer II
Timeline 2022
Services Strategy, Research, Design
Platform Web & Mobile

The Problem

Merchants were confused about Account Balance vs. Budget Remaining, leading to mistrust and poor budget planning.

  • Account Balance and Budget Remaining didn't match
  • Lack of transparency in campaign level spending
  • Merchants couldn't track campaign level spending
  • 30-day budget renewal cycle wasn't clearly communicated

The Solution

I created clear visual distinctions and surfaced campaign specific spending metrics to build merchant trust.

  • Tooltips distinguishing wallet from campaign budget
  • Campaign level spending breakdowns
  • Created funding alert system for low balances
  • Built statistics education modal for merchant clarity

Key Features

A closer look at the solutions that improved efficiency and user satisfaction.

Redesigned Dashboard

Unified view of Account Balance and Budget Remaining with clear visual separation and tooltips.

Campaign Details with Metrics

Granular spending breakdown by campaign, showing exactly where budget is allocated and spent in real time.

Add Funds Alert

Proactive notification system alerting merchants when Account Balance is low, preventing campaign interruptions.

Statistics Education Modal

Interactive guide explaining key metrics (impressions, clicks, ROAS) and how they relate to merchant budget and performance.

The Impact

Clear visual distinctions and transparent metrics transformed merchant trust and platform adoption.

The new design removed critical barriers that prevented merchants from effectively managing their advertising spend. By distinguishing wallet balance from campaign budgets with explanatory tooltips and surfacing granular spending breakdowns, merchants could finally see exactly where their money was going.

Proactive funding alerts prevented campaign interruptions, while the statistics education modal empowered merchants to make data-driven decisions. What was once a source of frustration became a trusted tool for growing their business.
